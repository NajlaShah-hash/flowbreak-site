Why I built FlowBreak

As the founder of FlowBreak, I created this solution after experiencing long, exhausting workdays that reduced my productivity and focus. I realized that short, structured breaks could transform how people work—helping them feel calmer, think more clearly, and complete meaningful work without burning out. FlowBreak is my practical toolkit for sustainable performance.

Simple, engaging, and effective

FlowBreak offers guided micro‑breaks, stretching exercises, and mindfulness prompts tailored for busy professionals. On desktop and mobile, FlowBreak provides gentle reminders to pause, breathe, and reset—so you return to your work with more energy and focus. Choose preset break rhythms (Pomodoro, 52/17, ultradian) or customize intervals to match your schedule. Built‑in progress tracking helps you notice patterns, maintain healthy habits, and prevent burnout over time.
